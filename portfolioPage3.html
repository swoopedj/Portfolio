About Myself

My name is Dylan Swoope and I am an aspiring Web Developer currently living in Austin, TX. I created this page for fun, practice, and reflection as I continue to explore new ideas and find new inspiration on my journey.

I currently work for the US Geological survey as a Hydrologic Technician doing surface water data collection. On a day to day basis I look at and ensure the quality of a large amount of environmental data.

My desire to learn to code has been burgeoning over the past couple of years and I am just now on the cusp of dedicating the better part of my time to persuing this desire.
